Boba Tea

Boba tea, or Bubble tea, is one of Taiwan's most famous cultural exports. The name of Bubble tea isn't from carbonation of the drink but from the chewy tapioca pearls that sit at the bottom of the cup. The first versions of the drink popped up in the 1980s in Taiwan's traditional tea shops. Now you can find a boba shop in almost every neighborhood.
